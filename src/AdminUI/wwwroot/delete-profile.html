How to Request Profile Deletion

Follow the steps below to request deletion of your profile.

Step 1: Open the App Menu

Tap the hamburger icon to open the menu

Step 2: Request Profile Deletion

Select the 'Delete Profile' menu option

Step 3: Confirm Your Request

You will be asked to confirm your request. Once you confirm, your profile deletion request will be submitted.

Step 4: Confirmation

You will see confirmation that your request has been submitted. After this you will be logged out of the app.

Step 5: Email

The SSW Rewards team will receive an email with your request. You will be copied on this email, so at this point if there has been an error, you have an opportunity to contact the team to correct it. Additionally, you can reply to this email requesting deletion of partial data rather than your complete profile. If the team can comply with this request they will, otherwise they will advise you that your request cannot be completed and ask you to confirm whether you wish to go ahead with the deletion of your complete profile.
